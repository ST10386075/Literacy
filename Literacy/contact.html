Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with Literacy Connect?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities including tutoring, mentoring, and administrative support. Please fill out our volunteer application form on the Volunteer Dashboard page, and our team will contact you with opportunities that match your skills and availability.

What types of donations do you accept?

We accept monetary donations through our secure online portal, as well as in-kind donations of books, educational materials, and technology equipment. Visit our Donation Dashboard for more information on current needs and how to contribute.

How do students sign up for tutoring?

Students or their parents/guardians can register through our Student Dashboard. After creating an account, they can browse available tutors, schedule sessions, and access learning resources.

Are your services free for students?

Yes, all our tutoring and educational resources are completely free for students, thanks to the generous support of our donors and volunteers.

How can I become a partner organization?

We're always looking to collaborate with schools, community centers, and other organizations. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@literacyconnect.org to discuss potential collaborations.
